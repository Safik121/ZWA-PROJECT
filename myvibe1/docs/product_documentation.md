# Product Documentation

## Introduction
**MyVibe** is a personal collection manager that allows you to track and organize your favorite media. Whether it's anime, movies, books, music, or recipes, MyVibe helps you keep everything in one place. You can also discover new content through our trending recommendations and share your collections with others.

## Getting Started

### Registration
To start using MyVibe, you need to create an account:
1.  Navigate to the **Login/Register** page.
2.  Click on **"Register here"** to flip the card to the registration form.
3.  Fill in your details:
    *   **Username**: Unique identifier (3-50 characters).
    *   **Email**: A valid email address.
    *   **Password**: At least 6 characters.
    *   **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password.
    *   **Avatar (Optional)**: Upload a profile picture (JPG, PNG, WEBP).
4.  Click **Register**. You will be automatically redirected to the login screen upon success.

### Login
1.  Enter your **Username** or **Email**.
2.  Enter your **Password**.
3.  Click **Log In**.

## Features

### Homepage (Trending)
The homepage displays trending content across various categories to give you inspiration for your collections.
*   **Categories**: Anime, Movies, Books, Music.
*   **Data Sources**: Real-time data from Jikan (Anime), TMDb (Movies), Google Books, and iTunes.
*   **Interactivity**: Click on any item to view more details in a popup.

### Collections
Collections are the core of MyVibe. You can create themed lists to organize your items.

#### Creating a Collection
1.  Go to the **Collections** page (via the navigation menu).
2.  Click the **"Edit"** button and then **"Create New Collection"** card.
3.  **Title**: Give your collection a name.
4.  **Theme**: Select a category (Games, Music, Movies, Books, Anime, Recipes, or Custom).
    *   *Note: The theme determines which API search tools will be available for adding items.*
5.  **Cover Image**: Optionally upload a cover image.
6.  Click **Create**.

#### Managing Items
Inside a collection, you can add items in two ways:
1.  **Search & Add (Recommended)**:
    *   Click the **"Add from [API]"** card (e.g., "Add from TMDb" for movies).
    *   Type the name of the item you are looking for.
    *   Select the correct item from the search results. It will be automatically added with its image and details.
2.  **Manual Add**:
    *   Click **"Add New Item"**.
    *   Fill in the Title, Description/Comment, and Rating (1-5 stars).
    *   Upload an image.
    *   Click **Add Item**.

### Profile
Your public profile showcases your identity and collections.
*   **Bio**: Write a short bio about yourself (editable).
*   **Recent Collections**: Displays your 3 most recent collections.
*   **Share**: You can share your profile URL with friends so they can see your collections.

### Search
Find other users to see what they are collecting.
*   Use the search bar in the header to search for users by username.
*   Click on a user profile to view their public collections.

## User Settings
Access your settings by clicking your avatar in the top right corner and selecting **Settings**.

*   **Change Avatar**: Update your profile picture.
*   **Change Display Name**: Update the name shown on your profile (separate from your unique username).
*   **Change Email**: Update your registered email address (requires current password).
*   **Change Password**: Update your login password.
*   **Delete Account**: Permanently delete your account and all associated data. **This action cannot be undone.**
